1. 部署token到sepolia：0x05E9eF776C1f7D13180C6EdF2bebf9f7f74B6E81
2. 部署tokenBank到sepolia：0x6AAEA242531A0a233BD9c9473DebB39b49417841
3. 部署autoWithdraw到sepolis：0xb169345DCcBfBADE10c37074d30bA2B6dEE4f9d0
